    Scuba Diving

    Reserve Now!

    Come explore the beautiful West Bay Beach in Honduras with the help of a PADI-certified diving instructor. With this Discover Scuba Diving program, you can try scuba diving without having to commit to a full certification course. And if you enjoy it, you can even apply your Discover Scuba Diving experience towards a PADI Open Water Diver certification. Come on down and explore the underwater world.

    Ziplining

    Reserve Now!

    Zipline through the lush Roatan jungle, visit the Monkey and Sloth Sanctuary, and take a dip at West Bay Tabyana Beach on a single action-packed tour. With a safety orientation and all equipment included, even beginners will feel comfortable flying through the forest canopy under the supervision of a guide.

    Tours

    Reserve Now!
